Peugeot 3008

Peugeot officially unveiled the new 3008 in May 2016, with the car being available as of January 2017. The car is due to be a new milestone for Peugeot, in its quest to become the highest selling range of vehicles in the industry.

Peugeot 508

The Peugeot 508 is a large family car launched in October 2010 by French automaker Peugeot, and followed by the 508 SW, an estate version, in March 2011.

Peugeot 308

The Peugeot 308 is a small family car produced by the French car manufacturer Peugeot. It was unveiled on 5 June 2007, and launched in September 2007.

Peugeot 2008

It's capable of over 50mpg and offers all the performance most drivers will need with plenty of refinement. Handling is neat and secure too, and while all 2008's are front-wheel drive, there's a clever traction control system called Advanced Grip Control to help keep you going in slippery conditions.

Peugeot 407

The Peugeot 407 is a large family car produced by the French automaker Peugeot, from 2003 to 2010. It was available in saloon, coupé and estate variants, with both diesel and petrol engines. The petrol engines ranged from 1.8 to 2.9 litres displacement, whereas the diesels ranged from 1.6 to 3.0 litre engines.
